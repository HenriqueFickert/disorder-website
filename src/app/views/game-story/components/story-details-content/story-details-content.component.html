In each world, a personification of the disorders takes control, tasked with guiding the player and hindering their progress through the created environment. These worlds are divided into specific stages, inspired by the most relevant feelings and symptoms of the chosen disorders, with the aim of allowing the player, in a lucid state, to experience the same sensations as individuals affected by those disorders.

Within these worlds, there are fractures that manifest as bright ruptures, symbolizing the connection between the world controlled by the disorder and the liberated world. In the case of the character Iio, these fractures serve as checkpoints, enabling him to maintain control of the situation as they represent locations where the disorder has less power.
